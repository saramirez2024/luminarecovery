Application Protocol

How to Use

  1. Cleanse skin thoroughly with a gentle, pH-balanced cleanser. Pat dry.
  2. Dispense 2-3 drops of Recovery Serum onto fingertips.
  3. Apply to target areas using gentle pressing motions — do not rub or drag across the skin.
  4. Allow 1-2 minutes for full absorption before layering additional products.
  5. Follow with a moisturizer or occlusive to seal in active ingredients.
  6. Use once daily in the evening, or as directed post-procedure.

Advanced Application

Microneedling Compatibility

This serum is formulated to be compatible with microneedling (collagen induction therapy). GHK-Cu is well-suited for post-microneedling application due to its tissue repair and anti-inflammatory signaling properties.

Needle Depth Guidelines

Depth Target Concern
0.25 mm Product absorption enhancement, superficial texture improvement
0.5 mm Fine lines, mild hyperpigmentation, overall skin rejuvenation
1.0 mm Moderate scarring, deeper wrinkles, collagen induction
1.5 mm Acne scars, deep wrinkles, stretch marks (professional setting recommended)

Timing Protocol

  • Apply serum immediately after microneedling while micro-channels are still open (within 5 minutes).
  • For depths over 1.0 mm, wait until pinpoint bleeding has stopped before applying.
  • Do not wash the treated area for at least 4 hours post-application.
  • Reapply serum the following morning and evening for 48-72 hours post-procedure.

Actives to Avoid Combining

  • Retinol / Retinoids — discontinue 3-5 days before microneedling and 3 days after.
  • Vitamin C (L-Ascorbic Acid) — avoid for 24-48 hours post-procedure due to pH sensitivity.
  • AHA/BHA exfoliants — discontinue 48 hours before and after microneedling.
  • Benzoyl Peroxide — avoid for 48 hours post-procedure (can cause excessive drying and irritation).

Do not use this product on:

  • Broken, sunburned, or compromised skin
  • Active acne lesions or pustules
  • Skin experiencing an active rosacea flare
  • Open wounds or freshly sutured areas
  • Areas with active eczema or dermatitis

If irritation, redness, or swelling occurs beyond expected post-procedure response, discontinue use and consult a dermatologist.

Care & Handling

Storage Instructions

  • Store in a cool, dry place between 15-25 °C (59-77 °F).
  • Keep away from direct sunlight and heat sources — amber glass provides UV protection but is not a substitute for proper storage.
  • Refrigeration is acceptable and may extend potency of the copper peptide complex.
  • Keep bottle tightly sealed when not in use to prevent oxidation.
  • Use within 6 months of opening.
